How To Add & Subtract!

To add, put two groups of things together to see how many you have in total, and to subtract, take some things away from a group to find out how many are left.

How To Multiply!

To multiply, think of it as putting together several groups of the same size to find out how many you have altogether.

How To Divide!

To divide, imagine sharing a group of things equally among a certain number of friends to see how many each friend gets.
